Popular Lebanese Dishes

Tabbouleh

Tabbouleh is a salad made with parsley, tomatoes, onions, bulgur wheat, lemon juice, and olive oil. It is a staple in Lebanese cuisine and is typically served as a side dish or mezze.

Hummus

Hummus is a dip made from mashed chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic. It is often served as an appetizer with pita bread, vegetables, or crackers.

Kibbeh

Kibbeh is a meatball made with ground lamb or beef, bulgur wheat, onions, and spices. It can be fried or baked and is typically served as a main course.
