Popular Tourist Attractions in Lebanon

Jeita Grotto

Jeita Grotto is a series of limestone caves located in the Nahr al-Kalb valley. It is a popular tourist destination and is known for its impressive stalactites and stalagmites.

Baalbek

Baalbek is an ancient city located in the Beqaa Valley. It is home to several well-preserved Roman ruins and is a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Byblos

Byblos is a historic port city located on the Mediterranean coast. It is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world and is home to several ancient ruins, including a Crusader castle and Roman amphitheater.

Getting around

Bus

There are many buses that do tours to popular locations and tourist attractions.

Car

The easiest way to get around outside of the cities is by car.

Weather

The weather in Lebanon is seasonal! It's best to come here during the spring to experience the nicest weather!
