Popular Lebanese Musicians

Fairuz

Fairuz is a legendary Lebanese singer who has been active since the 1950s. She is known for her soulful voice and her contributions to Arab music and culture.

Majida El Roumi

Majida El Roumi is another popular Lebanese singer who has been active since the 1970s. She is known for her romantic ballads and her powerful voice.

Ziad Rahbani

Ziad Rahbani is a composer and musician who is known for blending traditional Arabic music with jazz, rock, and other Western styles. He has released several albums and is a respected figure in the Lebanese music scene.
